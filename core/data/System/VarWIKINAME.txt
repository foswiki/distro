---+ WIKINAME -- your Wiki username
The WikiName is the same as =%<nop>USERNAME%= if not defined in the %USERSWEB%.%WIKIUSERSTOPIC% topic

   * =%<nop>WIKINAME%= expands to =%WIKINAME%=
