Foswiki is distributed under the [[http://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-2.0.html][Gnu General Public License]], and sources are freely available.

When you download an official Foswiki package from https://foswiki.org/Download, you automatically get most the Foswiki sources in the download package.

Foswiki developers collaborate online using =https://foswiki.org=, using the =Development= and =Tasks= webs, and on github. You can browse and download all the sources at https://github.com/foswiki
